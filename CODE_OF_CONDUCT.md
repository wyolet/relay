# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free
experience for everyone who participates — contributors, maintainers, and
users alike — regardless of background or identity.

## Our standard

We adopt the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as our code of
conduct. The full text defines the expected behavior, unacceptable
behavior, enforcement responsibilities, and enforcement guidelines:

- <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

In short: be respectful and constructive, assume good faith, accept
feedback gracefully, and focus on what's best for the community. Behavior
that harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated in any
project space (issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other
official channel).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the
project maintainers at **conduct@wyolet.com**. Reports will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy will be
respected.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and may take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to
any behavior they deem inappropriate, following the Contributor Covenant
2.1 enforcement guidelines.
